The Best Live Commerce Analytics System in BIDA

Our team is currently developing a live commerce analytics system for businesses engaged in e-commerce through live streaming platforms. This system provides real-time insights into viewership, engagement, and conversion rates, and use machine learning algorithms to offer personalized recommendations to improve user experience. We believe our system can help user overcome challenges and take advantage of opportunities in the live commerce industry.

Through extensive research and data gathering, we are confident that our system will provide valuable analytics for user to make informed decisions about their live commerce strategies. We are excited about the potential impact of our project and the opportunity to contribute to the future of e-commerce.
